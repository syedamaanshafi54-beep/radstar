Rad Star Trading Store Policies

Return & Refund Policy

Rad Star Trading is committed to ensuring your satisfaction with every purchase. Due to the nature of our food products, we do not accept returns on any items once they have been opened. However, if you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery to be eligible for a refund or replacement.

To initiate a refund/replacement request for a damaged or incorrect item, you must provide:

  • Your order number.
  • A clear photo or video of the damaged/incorrect product and the packaging.
  • A brief description of the issue.

Please send this information to our customer support at radstartrading@gmail.com or via WhatsApp at +91 90325 61974. Our team will review the case and, if approved, we will process your replacement or refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be processed to the original mode of payment where applicable.

Note: We do not offer refunds for issues related to personal taste or preference. All sales of non-damaged, correctly delivered items are final.

Shipping Policy

We are dedicated to delivering your products in a timely and secure manner. We currently ship to all serviceable pin codes within India.

  • Processing Time: Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Shipping Time: Delivery times vary based on your location. Standard shipping usually takes between 3-7 business days.
  • Shipping Charges: Shipping charges will be calculated at checkout based on your order value and location. We offer free shipping on orders above a certain value, which will be specified on the website.
  • Tracking: Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email and/or SMS with a tracking number and a link to track your package.

Rad Star Trading is not liable for any delays in delivery by the courier company. However, we will help you track your package and ensure it reaches you as soon as possible. In case of any unforeseen delays or issues, please contact our support team.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

  • Information We Collect: We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address when you place an order or create an account.
  • How We Use Your Information: Your information is used to process your orders, communicate with you about your purchases, and improve our services. We may also use your email to send you promotional offers, which you can opt-out of at any time.
  • Data Security: We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights and are required to keep the information confidential.
  • Third-Party Disclosure: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties unless we provide you with advance notice. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
