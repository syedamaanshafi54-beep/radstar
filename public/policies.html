However, if you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. We will be happy to arrange for a replacement or a full refund.

  • Contact us at radstartrading@gmail.com or call +91 90325 61974.
  • Please provide your order number and details of the issue.
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Shipping Policy

We currently ship to all locations within Hyderabad & Secunderabad. We are working on expanding our reach to serve more customers across India.

  • Processing Time: Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days.
  • Delivery Time: Once dispatched, orders are usually delivered within 2-4 business days within our service area.
  • Shipping Charges: We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹500. A nominal fee is charged for orders below this amount.
  • You will receive a tracking link via email once your order has been shipped.
